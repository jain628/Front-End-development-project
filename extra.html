Extra Curricular Activities

Chitkara University not just offers the best education but also grooms you for a well rounded personality.

There are two cultural clubs in Chitkara University namely Epic Youth Club (for hostellers)and C2S2 club(for hostellers and day scholars).

Epic Youth Club

EPIC stands for Enigmatic Persuers In Chitkara.It frequently organises competitions like Junoon and Civil war so that students can showcase their talents and helps them becoming an all rounder

C2S2 club

C2S2 stands for Chitkara Cultural and Social Services.It helps the children to show creative side of their personality through the events which are organised time to time.

C2S2 club has many subclubs:Rhythmic Soul-for dancing,Dhwani-for music,Awaaz-for theatre,Reflection- for art and craft,Panache-for modelling,Tasveer-for photography,MUN-for MUN,Bhangra Regiment-for Bhangra and Lethal Gidda Squad-for gidda.
