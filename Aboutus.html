Chitkara University

was founded by the Punjab State Legislature under "The Chitkara University Act" in 2010. The university comes under the establishment of the Chitkara Educational Trust based in Chandigarh and founded by Dr Ashok K Chitkara and Dr Madhu Chitkara in 1998
Rankings
University and college rankings
  • General – India
  • NIRF (Universities) (2018) 151–200
  • Pharmacy – India
  • NIRF (2018) 50
  • Chitkara University was ranked in the 151–200 band among universities in India by National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) in 2018 and 50 in the pharmacy ranking.

    • Campus
    Punjab Campus, Chitkara University Chitkara Educational Trust established the Punjab Campus in 2002 with initiation of Chitkara Institute of Engineering and Technology (CIET). The campus is spread over 50 acres, 30 kilometers from Chandigarh.[15]

    • International partners

    Chitkara University has partnership and tie-ups with education providers from countries like Australia, Brazil, Canada,
    China, France, Germany, Indonesia, Korea, Malaysia, Mexico, Netherlands, Spain, Switzerland, Taiwan, United States and UK.
    In U.S. Chitkara University has tie-ups with
    Central Michigan University, University of Florida, Purdue University, San Diego State University, University of Massachusetts, Lowell. In U.K. Chitkara has tie-up with Glasgow Caledonian University. In Canada the university is associated with George Brown College, Concordia University, University of Ontario Institute of Technology, University of Prince Edward Island (UPEI), Vancouver Island University. In Germany Chitkara has tie-up with International Summer University of Germany and Cologne Business School.
    In Australia, the university has tie-ups with Deakin University, Edith Cowan University, Flinders University.

    Chitkara university has global partnerships with Kyung Hee University, Korea University, Soongsil University etc.
