Hostel Facilities

  1. Separate residential facility for boys and girls.
  2. Common toilets for every floor.
  3. Rooms are alloted with beds(with mattresses),coolers,study table,chair,study lights.
  4. Power supply and free internet for 24 hours.
  5. Hot water is suplied in winters twice every day.
  6. Students are provided with kettles, irons, inductions,fridge,newspapers.
Every hostel has a garden so that students can stroll, exercise and relax.
University is committed to zero tolerance to ragging, alcohol, smoking, drugs and disciplinary misconduct.There is also a watch guard for the safety of hostel.Students are allowed to go outside campus by approving their gate pass approved by wardens.
The campus has a 24 hours hospital staffed with resident medical officers, medical lab, dieticians,ambulance.
Laundry services are available at a nominal charges.
Mess facility provides 3 meals a day while a snack is given at 5:00pm. Over 1 Lakh vegetarian meals are prepared each day!
